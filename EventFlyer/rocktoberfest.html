Rocktoberfest 2024

Schedule of Events

The event runs Thursday through Sunday and consists of sponsor booths with the latest gear, raffles and contests for prizes, movie night, live music, food trucks, climbing competitions, and clinics taught by professional athletes on Saturday and Sunday.

Free Admission & Camping for Volunteers

If you volunteer for one shift, you will get 50% off admission (to receive your 50% off admission, you will need to buy it at the registration booth when you check into your volunteer shift. You do not need to purchase a general admission and camping ticket online.) If you volunteer for two shifts, you get FREE admission into the event!
